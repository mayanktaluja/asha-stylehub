Good to know

Questions before you visit

Quick answers to what customers ask most. Anything else, message us on WhatsApp.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
Message us on WhatsApp for the day's timing, then walk in. A quick message means we are ready when you arrive.
How are measurements taken?
In person at the store, so the fit is right for you. Bring a garment that already fits you well as a reference if you can.
What should I bring for custom stitching?
Your fabric or a design photo, plus a reference garment if you have one. We guide you on the rest in store.
Can you stitch from fabric I already have?
Yes. Bring your own fabric, or choose from the range available in store.
Do you alter garments bought elsewhere?
Yes — bring the garment and we adjust the fit and finish it cleanly.
How long will my order take?
It depends on the garment and the season. We confirm an exact ready-date when you drop your fabric at the store.
How much does it cost?
Cost depends on the garment, fabric, and work involved. Message us on WhatsApp or ask in store and we share a clear quote.
Where are you, and how do I reach the store?
Jyoti Park, Main Road, near Aashirwad Marriage Lawn, Sector 7 Ext., Gurugram, Haryana. Tap “Open in Google Maps” below for directions.
